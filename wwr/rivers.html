Choose Your Adventure

Each of our rivers offers a unique experience. Whether you are looking for a family-friendly float or a big-water expedition, we have a trip that fits your group.

Main Fork of the Salmon River – Idaho

Known as the “River of No Return,” the Main Fork of the Salmon offers warm water, sandy beaches, and fun Class III rapids. It’s perfect for families and first-time rafters who still want plenty of excitement.

  • 6-day trips available
  • Class III rapids
  • Best season: June–August

Desolation and Gray Canyon – Utah

This remote stretch of the Green River flows through deep sandstone canyons and quiet desert landscapes. Long flatwater sections are mixed with fun splashy rapids, making it ideal for relaxing, scenic trips.

  • 5–6 day trips
  • Class II–III rapids
  • Great for groups and youth trips

Grand Canyon – Arizona

The legendary Grand Canyon combines world-class whitewater with towering canyon walls and unforgettable side hikes. This is the bucket-list trip for adventurers ready for bigger water and longer days.

  • 7–12 day expeditions
  • Class III–V rapids
  • Limited permits – book early
